Serving Humanity, Igniting Hope

Shri Swarajya Pratishthan

Chairman :- Nitin Deshmukh

Shri Swarajya Partishta is a noteworthy NGO that has dedicated itself to celebrating various forms of charitable work, which includes activities within local schools as well as special events commemorating significant dates such as October 2nd, 15-August, and 26-January etc. This organization serves as a beacon for philanthropy and social service, emphasizing the importance of giving back to the community and promoting altruistic endeavors. Shri Swarajya Partishta holds a coveted Central Government of India ETG 12a certificate, attesting to its commitment to transparency and adherence to legal regulations, making it a trusted and recognized entity in the realm of social work and charitable activities.
One of the notable aspects of this NGO is its focus on encouraging donations and supporting the noble cause of charitable work. Donors who contribute to Shri Swarajya Partishta not only play a vital role in furthering its mission but also benefit from tax deductions, thereby creating a mutually beneficial arrangement. This system of tax incentives serves as a powerful incentive for individuals and organizations to contribute to philanthropic causes, while also enabling them to reduce their tax liability. By fostering such a symbiotic relationship between donors and charitable organizations, Shri Swarajya Partishta continues to make a positive impact on society, making it a significant force for good in the community.
