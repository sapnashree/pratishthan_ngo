Shri Swarajya
Pratishthan, Nurturing
Hope Across Maharashtra.

The Shri Swarajya Pratishthan is a charitable organization registered under the charity laws. It was founded by Shri Nitin Prabhakarrao Deshmukh in 2018. Through the efforts of this organization, various initiatives are carried out in the state of Maharashtra. The primary focus of this organization is to provide services to the underprivileged in Maharashtra, irrespective of caste, religion, region, or any other social distinctions. Their goal is to uplift people economically, socially, and educationally at all levels.

Our Mission

To empower the underprivileged in Maharashtra, Shri Swarajya Pratishthan is committed to providing holistic support, transcending social barriers. Our mission is to uplift communities economically, socially, and educationally, fostering an inclusive society for sustainable growth.

Our Vision

Shri Swarajya Pratishthan envisions a compassionate and equitable Maharashtra, where every individual, regardless of background, has access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. We strive to break social distinctions, creating a brighter, united future for all.
